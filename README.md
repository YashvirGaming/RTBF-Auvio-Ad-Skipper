# RTBF-Auvio-Ad-Skipper
Take back control of your football match replays and live streams on RTBF Auvio! This lightweight extension is designed to automatically detect commercial ad breaks and fast-forward through them instantly, returning you to the game without the wait.

# Features:

Automatic Replay Ad Skipping: Instantly accelerates standard video-on-demand advertisement playback to 16x speed.
Live Stream Edge-Jumping: Detects ad buffers during live matches and automatically catches you up to the real-time live match edge.
Sleek Modern Dashboard: A beautiful dark-themed popup UI featuring a single-click master switch to toggle the skipper active or disabled.
Lifetime Statistics Tracking: Keeps a running counter of exactly how many ad breaks you have successfully skipped.
Privacy First: Zero data collection, tracking pixels, or remote logging. Everything happens securely on your own machine.
